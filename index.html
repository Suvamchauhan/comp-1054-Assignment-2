Facts:

  • LactoFun was created by a local Nepalese candy manufacture and is also known for its attractive packaging, which features bright colors and eye-catching designs.
  • LactoFun has been a popular candy brand in Nepal for many years and has become an important part of Nepalese food culture too.
  • LactoFun caramel candy is primarily known for its classic caramel flavor but nowadays it is available in different flavours like Chocolate, Coffee, Strawberry, Orange, Mint.
